About Us

Geometric Shapes and Applications is a project website created by M.Nisith. This website aims to provide information about various geometric shapes, their properties, and real-world applications. From 2D shapes like squares and circles to 3D shapes like cubes and spheres, this website covers a wide range of topics related to geometry.
